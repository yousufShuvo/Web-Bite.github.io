What Is Motion Graphics?

Motion graphics is animation with a strong emphasis on text. It's basically animated graphic design.
Since the inception of motion graphics, there has been a discussion regarding where the boundary between them and complete animation should be drawn. Hitchcock's Psycho's opening credits are an early example of motion graphics, in which the union of sound, motion, and graphic design works extraordinarily well. Motion graphics allow you to communicate with your audience while also adding depth to your message. They can send a message to us when combined with music and great copy. We use them to make advertisements, movie title sequences, [explainer](Motion graphics are animation with text as a main component.) videos, and to impart information. Motion graphics are used in many of our layouts to help you deliver your message.
